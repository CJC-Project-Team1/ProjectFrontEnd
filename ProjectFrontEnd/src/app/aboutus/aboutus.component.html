About Us

Our registered Non-Bank Finance Company, MSME Finance Services Ltd. (CFSL) assists SMEs, MSMEs and their founders to scale their businesses, through loan products in a variety of forms, structures and collaterals. Focusing on metros and Tier II cities, we service entities that are either in the stage of building up or diversifying, and generally not catered to by the mainstream banking system. We provide the right financing solution for business needs, driven solely by three key value propositions - flexibility, responsiveness and guidance.
